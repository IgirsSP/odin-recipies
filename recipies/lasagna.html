Recipe to cook a mouthwatering Lasagna

Lasagna

This is my Craig's mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Please follow and support the original contributors: homemade-lasagna

    Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Marzano tomatoes
  • oregano, chilli flakes, parsley and garlic powder
  • peeled garclic buds
  • 1 finely chopped onion

    Preparation

  1. Onwards with the recipe, first we will preapare the sauce
  2. In a pan kept on medium heat, add olive oil
  3. Add chopped garlic
  4. Toss till the garlic is cooked (not burnt), add onion to the pan
